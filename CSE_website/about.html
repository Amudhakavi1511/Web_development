Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a centre of excellence in Computer Science & Engineering, producing competent professionals and researchers with a global outlook.

Mission

  • To provide strong foundations in computing principles and practices.
  • To foster innovation and research in emerging areas.
  • To prepare students for leadership roles in industry and academia.

Accreditation & Recognition

The Department is accredited by NBA and recognized as a Research Centre by Anna University. It has a rich history of academic excellence, producing graduates who excel globally.
