Institutional Policies

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at SSN is committed to maintaining the highest standards of academic excellence, ethics, and student welfare. The following policies outline the guiding principles for students, faculty, and researchers.

All members are expected to uphold honesty in assignments, projects, and examinations. Plagiarism and academic dishonesty are strictly prohibited.

Students and faculty must demonstrate professionalism, respect, and inclusivity in all interactions. Any behavior that disrupts the academic environment is not tolerated.

Research must adhere to ethical practices, with respect for intellectual property, proper citation, and compliance with institutional and international standards.

Personal data collected by the institution is protected and used only for academic or administrative purposes in compliance with privacy regulations.

SSN strictly enforces a zero-tolerance policy against ragging. Students are encouraged to report any incidents immediately for swift action.
