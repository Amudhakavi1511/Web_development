Undergraduate Programs

The department offers a B.E. in Computer Science and Engineering, with a focus on foundational computing principles, problem-solving, and innovation.

  • B.E. CSE – 4 years
  • Industry-oriented curriculum
  • Internships & projects in collaboration with leading companies

Postgraduate Programs

Our postgraduate offerings provide advanced knowledge and specialization in emerging areas of computing.

  • M.E. Computer Science and Engineering – 2 years
  • M.E. Software Engineering – 2 years
  • Strong research focus with lab facilities

Doctoral Programs

The department is recognized as a Research Centre by Anna University and offers Ph.D. opportunities in diverse areas.

  • Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering
  • Collaborative research with industry and academia
  • Funding support through research grants
